## Description

Operation Triangulation is a mobile campaign targeting iOS devices. [SecureList OpTriangulation 01Jun2023](https://securelist.com/operation-triangulation/109842/) The unidentified actors used zero-click exploits in iMessage attachments to gain Initial Access, then executed exploits and validators, such as Binary Validator before finally executing the TriangleDB implant.
