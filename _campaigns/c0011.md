## Description

C0011 was a suspected cyber espionage campaign conducted by Transparent Tribe that targeted students at universities and colleges in India. Security researchers noted this campaign against students was a significant shift from Transparent Tribe's historic targeting Indian government, military, and think tank personnel, and assessed it was still ongoing as of July 2022. [Cisco Talos Transparent Tribe Education Campaign July 2022](https://blog.talosintelligence.com/2022/07/transparent-tribe-targets-education.html)
