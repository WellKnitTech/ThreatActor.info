## Description

Frankenstein was described by security researchers as a highly-targeted campaign conducted by moderately sophisticated and highly resourceful threat actors in early 2019. The unidentified actors primarily relied on open source tools, including Empire. The campaign name refers to the actors' ability to piece together several unrelated open-source tool components. [Talos Frankenstein June 2019](https://blog.talosintelligence.com/2019/06/frankenstein-campaign.html)
