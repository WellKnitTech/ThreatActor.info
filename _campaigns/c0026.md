## Description

C0026 was a campaign identified in September 2022 that included the selective distribution of KOPILUWAK and QUIETCANARY malware to previous ANDROMEDA malware victims in Ukraine through re-registered ANDROMEDA C2 domains. Several tools and tactics used during C0026 were consistent with historic Turla operations. [Mandiant Suspected Turla Campaign February 2023](https://www.mandiant.com/resources/blog/turla-galaxy-opportunity)
