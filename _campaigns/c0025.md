## Description

2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack was a Sandworm Team campaign during which they used Industroyer malware to target and disrupt distribution substations within the Ukrainian power grid. This campaign was the second major public attack conducted against Ukraine by Sandworm Team. [ESET Industroyer](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/Win32_Industroyer.pdf) [Dragos Crashoverride 2018](https://www.dragos.com/wp-content/uploads/CRASHOVERRIDE2018.pdf)
