## Description

The RedPenguin project was launched by Juniper in July 2024 to investigate reported malware infections of Juniper MX Series routers. RedPenguin activity was separately attributed to UNC3886 and included the deployment of multiple custom versions of the publicly-available TINYSHELL backdoor on Juniper routers. [Juniper RedPenguin MAR 2025](https://supportportal.juniper.net/sfc/servlet.shepherd/document/download/069Dp00000FzdmIIAR?operationContext=S1) [Mandiant UNC3886 Juniper Routers MAR 2025](https://cloud.google.com/blog/topics/threat-intelligence/china-nexus-espionage-targets-juniper-routers)
