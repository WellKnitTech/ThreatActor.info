## Description

FunnyDream was a suspected Chinese cyber espionage campaign that targeted government and foreign organizations in Malaysia, the Philippines, Taiwan, Vietnam, and other parts of Southeast Asia. Security researchers linked the FunnyDream campaign to possible Chinese-speaking threat actors through the use of the Chinoxy backdoor and noted infrastructure overlap with the TAG-16 threat group. [Bitdefender FunnyDream Campaign November 2020](https://www.bitdefender.com/files/News/CaseStudies/study/379/Bitdefender-Whitepaper-Chinese-APT.pdf) [Kaspersky APT Trends Q1 2020](https://securelist.com/apt-trends-report-q1-2020/96826/) [Recorded Future Chinese Activity in Southeast Asia December 2021](https://go.recordedfuture.com/hubfs/reports/cta-2021-1208.pdf)
