## Description

The J-magic Campaign was active from mid-2023 to at least mid-2024 and featured the use of the J-magic backdoor, a custom cd00r variant tailored for use against Juniper routers. The J-magic Campaign targeted Junos OS routers serving as VPN gateways primarily in the semiconductor, energy, manufacturing, and IT sectors. [Lumen J-Magic JAN 2025](https://blog.lumen.com/the-j-magic-show-magic-packets-and-where-to-find-them/)
