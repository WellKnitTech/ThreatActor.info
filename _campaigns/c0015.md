## Description

C0015 was a ransomware intrusion during which the unidentified attackers used Bazar, Cobalt Strike, and Conti, along with other tools, over a 5 day period. Security researchers assessed the actors likely used the widely-circulated Conti ransomware playbook based on the observed pattern of activity and operator errors. [DFIR Conti Bazar Nov 2021](https://thedfirreport.com/2021/11/29/continuing-the-bazar-ransomware-story/)
