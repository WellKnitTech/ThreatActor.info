## Description

Operation MidnightEclipse was a campaign conducted in March and April 2024 that involved initial exploit of zero-day vulnerability CVE-2024-3400, a critical command injection vulnerability in the GlobalProtect feature of Palo Alto Networks PAN-OS. [Volexity UPSTYLE 2024](https://www.volexity.com/blog/2024/04/12/zero-day-exploitation-of-unauthenticated-remote-code-execution-vulnerability-in-globalprotect-cve-2024-3400/) [Palo Alto MidnightEclipse APR 2024](https://unit42.paloaltonetworks.com/cve-2024-3400/)
