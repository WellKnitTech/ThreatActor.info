## Description

Adversaries may perform a port scan on a system, device, or network to identify live hosts, enumerate open ports and running services, identify operating systems, and map out the network. [NIST SP 800-82r3](https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/SpecialPublications/NIST.SP.800-82r3.pdf) The results of a port scan may inform adversary Discovery, Lateral Movement, and vulnerability exploitation decisions (Exploitation for Evasion, Exploitation for Privilege Escalation, Exploitation of Remote Services). 

Some common tools for executing a port scan include `nmap`, `netcat`, and the Advanced Port Scanner.
