## Description

An adversary may compress or encrypt data that is collected prior to exfiltration using a custom method. Adversaries may choose to use custom archival methods, such as encryption with XOR or stream ciphers implemented with no external library or utility references. Custom implementations of well-known compression algorithms have also been used. [ESET Sednit Part 2](http://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/eset-sednit-part-2.pdf)
