## Description

Wireless signals frequently propagate outside of organizational boundaries, which provide opportunities for adversaries to monitor or gain unauthorized access to the wireless network. [CISA March 2010](https://us-cert.cisa.gov/ncas/tips/ST05-003) To minimize this threat, organizations should implement measures to detect, understand, and reduce unnecessary RF propagation. [DHS National Urban Security Technology Laboratory April 2019](https://www.dhs.gov/sites/default/files/saver-msr-rf-detection_cod-508_10july2019.pdf)
