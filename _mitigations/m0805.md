## Description

Utilize a layered protection design based on physical or mechanical protection systems to prevent damage to property, equipment, human safety, or the environment. Examples include interlocks, rupture disk, release values, etc. [A G Foord, W G Gulland, C R Howard, T Kellacher, W H Smith 2004](https://www.icheme.org/media/9906/xviii-paper-23.pdf)
