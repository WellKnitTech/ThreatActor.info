## Introduction
Sidewinder (also known as T-APT-04 or Rattlesnake) is a suspected Indian threat actor group that has been active since at least 2012. They have been observed targeting government, military, and business entities throughout Asia, primarily focusing on Pakistan, China, Nepal, and Afghanistan.

## Activities and Tactics
Sidewinder targets various sectors:

### Primary Targets
- Government agencies
- Military organizations
- Defense contractors
- Business entities

### Key Motivations
- Intelligence gathering
- Strategic access
- Regional operations

### Geographic Focus
- Pakistan
- China
- Nepal
- Afghanistan

## Notable Campaigns
1. **Regional Operations**: Ongoing Asia-targeted campaigns
2. **Government Targeting**: Compromising government entities
3. **Military Focus**: Targeting defense organizations

## Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
Sidewinder is known for the following TTPs:

### Initial Access
- **Spear Phishing**: Targeted email campaigns
- **Watering Hole Attacks**: Compromising legitimate sites

### Execution
- **Command and Scripting Interpreter**
- **PowerShell**
- **VBScript**

### Persistence
- **Scheduled Tasks**
- **Registry Keys**

### Defense Evasion
- **Obfuscation**

### Discovery
- **System Information Discovery**

### Collection
- **Screen Capture**
- **Keylogging**
