## Introduction
Nova appears to refer to a ransomware-as-a-service ("RaaS") infrastructure provider. Public threat reporting indicates that affiliates leveraging the Nova victim extortion site are known to use a Rust-based ransomware developed by the RALord ransomware operation, although an original ".nova" ransomware family has been identified as well.<sup>[[SonicWall Nova Ransomware April 11 2025](/references/4926be5f-0eea-44cc-a73e-2f173eee901b)]</sup><sup>[[Cyble April 17 2025](/references/cad46e02-2e68-4347-be1c-7be910adee95)]</sup>
