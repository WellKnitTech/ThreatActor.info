## Introduction
CyberVolk is a politically motivated hacktivist collective originating in India with pro-Russia leanings. The group is known for launching ransomware and DDoS attacks to undermine and disrupt operations of entities that are perceived to be opposed to Russian interests.<sup>[[SentinelOne November 25 2024](/references/71c8e60c-a72a-4bff-aae3-f3f155fa22ee)]</sup>
