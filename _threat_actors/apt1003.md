## Introduction
Ember Bear (also known as Cadet Blizzard or DEV-0586) is a Russian state-sponsored cyber espionage group that has been active since at least 2020, linked to Russia's General Staff Main Intelligence Directorate (GRU) 161st Specialist Training Center (Unit 29155).

Ember Bear has primarily focused operations against Ukrainian government and telecommunication entities, but has also operated against critical infrastructure entities in Europe and the Americas. The group conducted the WhisperGate destructive wiper attacks against Ukraine in early 2022.

## Activities and Tactics
Ember Bear conducts targeted operations:

### Primary Targets
- Ukrainian government agencies
- Telecommunications organizations
- Critical infrastructure
- Energy sector

### Key Motivations
- Intelligence gathering
- Destructive attacks
- Disruption of critical services

### Geographic Focus
- Ukraine (primary)
- Europe
- Americas

## Notable Campaigns
1. **WhisperGate Attacks (2022)**: Destructive wiper attacks against Ukraine
2. **Ukrainian Government Operations**: Targeting government entities
3. **Telecommunications Targeting**: Compromising telecom providers
4. **Critical Infrastructure Attacks**: Targeting energy and utilities
