## Introduction
UNC3784 is a suspected Chinese espionage actor, which - alongside other China-backed groups - was observed exploiting the "Follina" vulnerability (CVE-2022-30190) in zero-day attacks on organizations in Russia and Asia. UNC3784 specifically was observed deploying backdoor and downloader malware on compromised government networks in Southeast Asia.<sup>[[Mandiant M-Trends 2023](/references/fabb9f5c-3ce6-4eef-8711-13130ff41884)]</sup>
