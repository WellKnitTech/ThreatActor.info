## Introduction
APT39 is one of several names for cyber espionage activity conducted by the Iranian Ministry of Intelligence and Security (MOIS) through the front company Rana Intelligence Computing since at least 2014.

APT39 has primarily targeted the travel, hospitality, academic, and telecommunications industries in Iran and across Asia, Africa, Europe, and North America to track individuals and entities considered to be a threat by the MOIS.

## Activities and Tactics
APT39 conducts surveillance-focused operations:

### Primary Targets
- Travel industry
- Hospitality organizations
- Academic institutions
- Telecommunications companies
- individuals of interest

### Key Motivations
- Surveillance of dissidents
- Intelligence gathering
- Tracking individuals

### Geographic Focus
- Iran
- Asia
- Africa
- Europe
- North America

## Notable Campaigns
1. **Travel Industry Targeting**: Compromising travel service providers
2. **Hospitality Sector Attacks**: Targeting hotels and travel agencies
3. **Telecommunications Operations**: Compromising telecom providers

## Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
APT39 is known for the following TTPs:

### Initial Access
- **Spear Phishing**: Targeted email campaigns
- **Watering Hole Attacks**: Compromising legitimate sites

### Execution
- **Command and Scripting Interpreter**
- **PowerShell**

### Persistence
- **Scheduled Tasks**
- **Registry Keys**

### Defense Evasion
- **Obfuscation**

### Discovery
- **System Information Discovery**
- **Account Discovery**

### Collection
- **Screen Capture**
- **Keylogging**
