## Introduction
The SafePay ransomware group is a cybercrime gang known for deploying ransomware to encrypt files and demand ransoms. They are relatively obscure and not widely discussed on illicit forums. Their activities include data exfiltration and file encryption, often using tools like WinRAR and FileZilla for archiving and potential exfiltration.<sup>[[huntress.com November 14 2024](/references/0418012c-af7e-47b0-b690-85fd634532e4)]</sup>
