## Introduction
FIN6 (also known as Magecart Group 6 or Skeleton Spider) is a cybercrime group that has stolen payment card data and sold it for profit on underground marketplaces. This group has aggressively targeted and compromised point of sale (PoS) systems in the hospitality and retail sectors.

FIN6 is known for the Magecart attacks that compromise payment card data at scale.

## Activities and Tactics
FIN6 focuses on financial theft:

### Primary Targets
- Hospitality industry
- Retail sector
- Point-of-sale systems

### Key Motivations
- Financial gain
- Payment card data theft
- Sale of stolen data

### Geographic Focus
- Global operations
- Primarily targets US and European entities

## Notable Campaigns
1. **Magecart Operations**: Large-scale payment card skimming
2. **PoS System Compromises**: Targeting retail and hospitality
3. **Hospitality Targeting**: Hotel and restaurant chains

## Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
FIN6 is known for the following TTPs:

### Initial Access
- **Spear Phishing**: Targeted email campaigns
- **Exploit Public-Facing Applications**

### Execution
- **Command and Scripting Interpreter**
- **PowerShell**

### Persistence
- **Scheduled Tasks**
- **Registry Keys**

### Defense Evasion
- **Obfuscation**: Hiding malicious code

### Discovery
- **System Information Discovery**

### Collection
- **Screen Capture**
- **Keylogging**
- **Clipboard Data**

### Exfiltration
- **Exfiltration Over C2 Channel**
