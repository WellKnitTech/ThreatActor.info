## Introduction
Anonymous is a decentralized international group of hacktivists known for carrying out cyber attacks against government bodies and corporations worldwide. Their activities often include distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks, website defacements, and data leaks, primarily motivated by opposition to internet censorship, control, and various geopolitical issues.<sup>[[Cyber Security Agency of Singapore March 9 2022](/references/382b5b53-8f24-4e20-9493-ccc8187fef51)]</sup>
